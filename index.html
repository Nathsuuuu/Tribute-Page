Arnold Schwarzenegger

Arnold Schwarzenegger is an Austrian-American actor, producer, businessman, retired bodybuilder, and former politician who served as the 38th Governor of California from 2003 to 2011. He began weight training at the age of 15 and won the Mr. Universe title at age 20 and went on to win the Mr. Olympia contest seven times. Schwarzenegger has remained a prominent figure in the fitness and bodybuilding community, inspiring many with his achievements.

Apart from his bodybuilding career, Schwarzenegger is best known for his roles in blockbuster films such as the Terminator series, Predator, and Total Recall. He has also been involved in various philanthropic activities and initiatives, including campaigns against climate change.
